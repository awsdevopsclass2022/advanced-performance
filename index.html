WHAT IS CHIP TUNING OR ECU REMAP?

ECU Remapping (Electronic Control Unit tuning) is a process of changing product factory settings made by manufacturers and optimize the settings to add extra performance. Car remapping offers many options that can be improved or remove things like RMP power restrictions, better fuel efficiency, or increase absolute boost and boost delivery. New technology will make your car better and easier to drive.
